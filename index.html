About

I'm a founder and operations professional with over a decade of experience spanning banking and finance, product-based entrepreneurship, and hospitality management. I founded DKNS Marketing in 2011, sourcing and exporting premium Sri Lankan tea and cinnamon, and later co-founded South Scooters and Daffodils Hill Cabana, a scooter rental service and vacation villa in Galle that I manage remotely from Australia.

Before entrepreneurship became my full-time focus, I spent almost 11 years at Senkadagala Finance PLC, rising to Manager and overseeing credit evaluation, recoveries, and branch operations. Since relocating to Australia, I've been broadening my skill set across food safety and quality (HACCP, GMP, ISO 22000), manufacturing operations, real estate, and mortgage broking — building a versatile, cross-industry foundation for what's next.
